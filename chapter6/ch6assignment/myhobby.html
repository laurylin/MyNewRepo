Ballroom Dancing

Ballroom dancing is more than movement; it’s storytelling. Every step, turn, and expression connects rhythm, discipline, and heart. I compete in multiple styles, from waltz to cha-cha, and I love the way dance combines artistry and athleticism.

What makes it truly meaningful to me is how I invite God into it. My goal is to reflect His creativity and excellence through every performance, showing that grace and strength can exist in the same space.
