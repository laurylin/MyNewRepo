Favorite Releases

Five Nights at Freddy’s (2014)
This breakout hit became a viral anthem in the gaming community. It blends electronic beats with creepy, haunting lyrics that perfectly match the FNaF atmosphere.
No Mercy (2016)
Inspired by the game Overwatch, this track is playful yet chaotic. It captures the frustration and humor of gaming culture with sharp lyrics and addictive rhythm.
Zero_One (2019 Album)
Their first original full-length album showcases their electronic rock style beyond fan-inspired songs. It’s innovative, experimental, and shows their range as artists.
